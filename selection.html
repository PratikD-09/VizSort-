Selection Sort

Description

Selection Sort is a simple sorting algorithm that divides the input list into two parts: the sublist of items already sorted and the sublist of items remaining to be sorted.


It repeatedly selects the minimum element from the unsorted portion and moves it to the beginning of the unsorted portion until the entire list is sorted.

Complexity

Average Complexity O(n^2)
Best Case O(n^2)
Worst Case O(n^2)
Space Complexity O(1)
